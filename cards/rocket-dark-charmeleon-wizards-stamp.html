Team Rocket Dark Charmeleon Kraft Giveaway Wizards Stamp

There is a gold "W" for Wizards of the Coast stamped on the card.

rare

Rare Version

This card was given away as part of a Kraft Foods Giveaway in Australia. This "W Stamped" card is harder to find compared to others due to the limited distribution.

The PSA graded cards include "2001 Pokemon GM. Promo" and "Kraft Gvwy. - Wiz. Gold Stamp" to identify the variant.
