Pokemon Center NY Stamped Black Star Promos

There is a "Pokemon Center NY" stamp on Ho-oh and Rapidash.

The rare version of this card has a "Pokemon Center NY" stamp underneath the illustration. There were the first distribution and given out at the NYC Pokemon Store.

Another version lacking the stamp was available as an insert with the November 2002 issue of Nintendo Power magazine.
