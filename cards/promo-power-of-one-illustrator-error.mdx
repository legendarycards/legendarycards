These cards were distributed as part of the "Power of One" movie release.

The incorrect illustrator, Toshinao Aoki, was printed on these cards for most of the distribution.

PSA will list the more common version as "Aoki Error" or without a specific variant indicator.

A final run with the corrected illustrator, Naoyo Kimura, happend in the UK.
